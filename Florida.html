Day 1: Florida Keys Adventure

Morning: Drive to Key West

Start early from your hotel in Homestead (around 7:00 AM) to make the most of your day. The scenic drive to Key West takes approximately 3.5 hours without stops, but you'll want to admire the breathtaking views along the way.

Scenic Drive Highlights:

  • Seven Mile Bridge - one of the most spectacular bridges in the world
  • Overseas Highway - driving over crystal blue waters
  • Multiple Keys islands with unique characteristics

The stunning Overseas Highway connecting the Florida Keys

Afternoon: Key West Exploration

Lunch at Blue Heaven

Start with a delicious brunch at this outdoor restaurant known for its Caribbean flair and roosters roaming freely.

$15-25 per person 729 Thomas St, Key West

Budget tip: Split the famous Lobster Benedict ($22) or opt for the Rooster Special ($14.50) with eggs, potatoes, and bacon

Ernest Hemingway Home and Museum

Experience the historic home where the famous author lived and wrote for more than 10 years.

$19 per adult 907 Whitehead St, Key West

Enjoy the beautiful gardens and meet the six-toed cats that are descendants of Hemingway's pets

The historic Ernest Hemingway Home and Museum

Evening: Budget-Friendly Key West Activities

FREE

Dinner at The Stoned Crab

$20-30 per person

Day 2: Everglades National Park & Turtle Conservation

Morning: Everglades National Park

Drive from Homestead to Everglades National Park (very close to your hotel, about 15 minutes).

Everglades National Park Entrance

$35 per vehicle Valid for 7 days

Budget tip: This is a per-vehicle fee, so the cost is split among all passengers

Must-Visit Trails:

  • Anhinga Trail - 0.8-mile loop with abundant wildlife viewing
  • Gumbo Limbo Trail - shaded forest path with unique vegetation
  • Pa-hay-okee Overlook - panoramic views of the "River of Grass"

Budget tip: Pack a picnic lunch to enjoy in the park

Amazing wildlife encounters in the Everglades National Park

Afternoon: Marathon Area

Drive to Marathon in the Middle Keys (approximately 1.5 hours from the Everglades).

The Turtle Hospital

Take an educational tour to learn about sea turtle conservation efforts.

$30 per adult Tours run every half-hour, 9am-4pm

Budget tip: This is an affordable activity that supports conservation efforts

Old Seven Mile Bridge

FREE

Walk or cycle across this historic bridge offering spectacular views

The Turtle Hospital in Marathon helps rescue and rehabilitate sea turtles

Evening: Sunset Grille & Raw Bar

Enjoy dinner with stunning views of the Seven Mile Bridge.

$15-25 per person 7 Knights Key Blvd, Marathon

Budget tip: Their sushi platters are generous and can be shared. Don't miss trying Key Lime Pie for dessert!

Money-Saving Tips

Share Meals

Florida portions are often generous, especially at seafood restaurants. Consider sharing an entrÃ©e and maybe an appetizer.

Bring Your Own Gear

If you plan to snorkel, bring your own gear rather than renting it ($15-20 savings per person).

Refillable Water Bottles

Bring refillable water bottles to stay hydrated in the Florida heat (savings of $3-4 per bottle).

Happy Hour Specials

Look for happy hour specials at restaurants and bars, especially in Key West (savings of 25-50%).

Carpool

Share transportation costs if traveling with friends to split gas and parking fees.

Pack Snacks

Pack snacks for your road trips to avoid expensive convenience store purchases.

Final Travel Tips

Weather Preparedness

Florida can be hot and sudden rain showers are common. Bring sunscreen, a hat, and a light rain jacket.

Insect Protection

Especially important for the Everglades. Bring insect repellent to keep mosquitoes away.

Photo Opportunities

Don't miss the sunset at Mallory Square in Key West or the panoramic views from the Seven Mile Bridge.

Enjoy your Florida adventure! This beautiful region offers a unique blend of natural beauty, wildlife, and culture that will create lasting memories.
